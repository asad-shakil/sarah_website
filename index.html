The people behind the Sarah Institute

Sarah Institute of eGeneration was founded on 7th August 2015 by Atiqur Rahman Molla who is also the founder of Outsource Experts Ltd. who today employs more than 300 people on multiple locations worldwide.

The name for the Institute, and the idea behind the institute is a tribute to Atiqur's grandmother Sarah who was a persistent and great believer in education.
